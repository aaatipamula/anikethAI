# Game Layout

You are dealt 5 cards from the deck, you can choose a suit, rank, or specific card that you think will appear in your dealt hand.

# Multipliers

If you get the suit, a 2x multiplier is applied to your gambled amount
If you get the rank, a 4x multiplier is applied to your gambled amount
If you get the card, an 8x multiplier is applied to your gambled amount
