## Game Layout

This is an extremely simple card-based gambling game.
Each new player starts out with `$500` Moner in their bank account (not real money).
Players can reload their accounts with `$250` Moner every 24 hours.

> Read the help pages for `bank` and `bank reload` to view and manage Moners.

## Gameplay

1. You can pick any amount to gamble up to you bank balance.
2. You are dealt **4** cards from a standard 52 card deck.
3. You can choose a *suit*, *rank*, or *specific card* that you think will appear in your dealt hand.
4. If you successfully guessed the *suit*, *rank*, or *card* you will be awarded some multiple of your gambled amount (see below).

## Multipliers

Correct `Suit` -> `2x` gambled amount
Correct `Rank` -> `4x` gambled amount
Correct `Card` -> `8x` gambled amount
